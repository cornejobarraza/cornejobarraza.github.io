So what is this?

Good question! I've always been drawn to computers and tech in general. I had the opportunity to study an associate's degree on networking and security, which I had always thought was my dream career path most of my life. Fast forward a couple of years and I'm about to graduate from a career that even though is very profitable, it doesn't make me feel as excited as I thought it would.

During this time of questioning my decisions and wondering what the future might have in-store for me, I catched up with a good friend after we lost contact for a couple of years. We talked about life and stuff, but at some point he told me he had become a web developer and for some reason that resonated a lot with me.

I have since started a journey to become a developer too, and this website is my way to show myself and other people what I've accomplished so far. I'm pretty sure something changed that day, as a sudden drive for learning new things kicked in again. The page will be in constant change but I'll make sure every part of it is well explained.
