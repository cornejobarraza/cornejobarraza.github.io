So what is this?

Good question! Well, I've always been drawn to computers and tech in general, and I recently had the chance to study an associate's degree on networking and security. I had always seen this as my dream career path, but fast forward a couple of years and I'm about to graduate from a career that even though is very profitable, doesn't make me feel as excited as I thought it would.

During this time of questioning my vocation and wondering what the future might hold for me, I catched up with a good friend I had lost contact with a long ago. We talked about life and what not, but at some point he told me he had gotten a job as a developer and for some reason, that stuck with me.

I have since started a journey to become a developer too, and this website is my way to show myself and others what I've accomplished so far. I'm pretty sure something changed for me that day, as a sudden drive for learning new things kicked back in. The page will constantly change as I improve, but I'll make sure everything here makes sense.
